## What this skill is for

One or two sentences on the job this skill does and when to reach for it. The
`description` field above is what the agent uses to decide relevance, so keep it
specific and trigger-oriented ("Use when…").

## Working rules

- Bullet the concrete steps or constraints the assistant should follow.
- Be imperative and unambiguous — skills run without you in the loop.
